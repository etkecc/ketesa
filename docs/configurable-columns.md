# Configurable table columns

Every data table in Ketesa lets you choose which columns are shown and what order they appear in. Instead of giving each table its own settings control, Ketesa has a single configure mode: switch it on from the app bar and you can rearrange any table on the page.

## Adjusting a table's columns

The gear icon in the top app bar turns configure mode on. Click the table you want to change and a panel opens with its columns: flip a column's switch to show or hide it, drag the entries to reorder them. The table updates as you go, no save button required. Click the gear icon again when you're done.

Configuration is desktop-only. On phones and narrow windows tables collapse to a simplified single-column list with no gear icon, so there's nothing to arrange there.

## Where your settings live

Your choices go into the browser's local storage rather than the server, which keeps them specific to the browser and device you set them on. A layout you arrange on your desktop won't follow you to your phone or another browser, and clearing the site's browsing data resets every table back to its defaults.
